Francesco Pittaluga

Home Resume Bio

Francesco Pittaluga was born to peruvian parents, in Miami, Fl on December 6, 1991. Until he was five years old, Francesco spent half the year living in Peru and half the year living in Miami. At the age of five, he enrolled in Alexander Montessori School where he attended until the sixth grade. For middle school, Francesco transferred to Ransom Everglades where he attended until the twelfth grade. After graduating high school, Francesco moved to Boston and enrolled at Tufts University where he is currently double majoring in Computer Science and Computer Engineering.
